Under The Sky

Sept 23rd - Oct 22nd

Libra

Astrology and superstitions have always been part of the way humans search for meaning beyond what we can see. They’re less about predicting fate and more about the quiet ways we stay hopeful, connected and grounded when life feels uncertain.

The alignment of planets, the shifting of the moon or the traits tied to zodiac signs may seem abstract, yet they offer us a symbolic language to understand ourselves and our place in the universe.

Even today, in a fast-paced world that often dismisses old beliefs, these traditions linger. They live in the objects we keep close, the habits we repeat without thinking and the reflections we return to in quiet moments. Pause for a moment, look around you. What small symbol, ritual or habit quietly guides you today in ways you barely notice. Whether you believe deeply or simply enjoy the journey, astrology and superstitions remind us of something universal, our enduring need for connection, meaning and balance and that is exactly what this season invites us to reflect on.

"The Balance of Libra"

This week, the sun rests in Libra, the sign of balance, harmony and connection. Libra reminds us that life is not meant to be lived in extremes, it’s the in-between spaces, the pauses and the conversations that bring us closer to understanding ourselves and others.

Astrology often gets mistaken as a prediction machine, but at its heart, it’s a mirror. When we look at Libra, we’re not just looking at “traits of a zodiac sign.” We’re being reminded of the universal need for balance: between work and rest, speaking and listening, giving and receiving.

Think about the rituals you already have, maybe knocking on wood when you’re nervous, or carrying a charm for good luck. These small acts, like Libra’s balancing scales, are ways we quietly negotiate with the unknown. They help us feel steady in a world that never stops moving.

Your Libra Check-In for the Week
Notice the balance in your life: are you giving more than you receive, or receiving more than you give? Where can you invite more harmony?
Reflect on your voice: have you stayed quiet when you needed to speak, or spoken when listening might have served you better?
Look for the hidden designs in your day, the symbols, habits or rituals that quietly ground you.
Try this

Choose one small ritual to invite balance into this week. It could be as simple as taking three deep breaths before starting your day, writing a short note of gratitude at night or even knocking on wood when uncertainty creeps in. Pay attention to how this little act shifts your energy and brings you back to center.

Libra whispers that balance isn’t about perfection, it’s about alignment. Even the smallest rituals, from checking your horoscope to tossing salt over your shoulder, remind us that we’re always seeking harmony between what we see and what we feel.
